Residential Complex

Al-Shorouk Development

Cairo, Egypt

Back to Projects

Al-Shorouk Development represents a harmonious blend of modern architecture and traditional Egyptian design elements. This residential complex features spacious apartments, landscaped gardens, and community spaces designed to foster a sense of neighborhood while providing privacy and comfort.

The project incorporates sustainable design principles, including natural ventilation systems, solar orientation for optimal daylight, and water-efficient landscaping. Each unit is thoughtfully designed to maximize living space while maintaining a connection to the surrounding environment.
