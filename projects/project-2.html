Residential Interior

Modern Living Space

New Cairo, Egypt

Back to Projects

A contemporary residential interior showcasing minimalist aesthetics with artistic flair. This living space features custom furniture, unique sculptural elements, and a carefully curated color palette that creates a serene yet sophisticated atmosphere.

The design emphasizes clean lines, natural materials, and strategic use of mirrors to enhance spatial perception. Every element has been selected to contribute to a cohesive, harmonious living environment.
